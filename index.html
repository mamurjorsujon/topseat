About the Fork

Welcome to TheFork! At TheFork.se we offer you over 30,000 restaurants available at any time and at the best price in Sweden as well as in several other countries around Europe. Are you looking for romantic restaurants in Stockholm , trendy restaurants in Gothenburg , maybe a cozy Sunday brunch in Stockholm or are you perhaps eager to discover a completely new gastronomic place somewhere in Sweden?

Get inspired to find the best restaurants just for you! Follow the guide on TheFork.se.

When you book with TheFork, you can also take advantage of a discount of up to 50% at several of the fine restaurants.
